# "The Digital Nomad Packing List 2026: 47 Items I Actually Use After 2 Years of Full-Time Travel"

Every "digital nomad packing list" on the internet is written by someone who packed once. This one is refined after 2 years of full-time travel across 20+ countries — with real weight, real utility, and real regrets about what I didn't bring.

Key points:
- The philosophy: pack for 1 week, wash often, buy locally what you need
- The exact backpack and luggage setup (with weights)
- Tech gear that actually matters vs stuff you think you need
- Clothing strategy for different climates in one bag
- The "I wish I had this from day one" items
- What to skip: the 20% of gear you carry but never use
- Country-specific additions: adapters, SIM tools, water filters
- How to pack for a 2-week workation vs a 6-month nomad stint
- The digital nomad pharmacy: medications and health items to carry
